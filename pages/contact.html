FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the site visit really free?

Yes, completely free of charge and with no obligation. Our expert visits your home, takes measurements, shows you samples, and provides a quotation — all at zero cost.

How long does installation take?

Most residential projects are completed in 1–3 days depending on size and products. We will give you a precise timeline with your quotation.

Do you serve areas outside Kochi?

We serve all major cities in Kerala including Thrissur, Thiruvananthapuram, Kozhikode, Kottayam, and more. Call us for locations outside our primary service area.

What is the minimum order value?

There is no minimum order. We handle single-room projects as well as full-home and large commercial transformations with the same care and professionalism.

Do you offer EMI or payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options including EMI through select payment partners. Discuss this with our team and we will arrange what works for you.

Is there a warranty on your products?

All products come with manufacturer warranties. Installation work is additionally backed by our own 1-year service guarantee. We stand behind everything we do.
