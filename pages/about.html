Who We Are

More Than a Furnishing Store

HOMSPACE was born from a simple frustration — furnishing a home in Kerala was fragmented, overwhelming, and often disappointing. Customers had to visit ten different shops, coordinate multiple vendors, and still ended up with results that didn't match their vision.

"We decided to build the one place that handles it all — beautifully."

Today, HOMSPACE is Kerala's single destination for complete home furnishing — wallpaper, wall panels, curtains, blinds, partition doors, and mosquito nets. One partner, one seamless experience, zero hassle.

We've completed over 1,000 transformations across Kerala — from cozy apartments in Kochi to luxury villas in Trivandrum and commercial spaces in Thrissur. Every project handled with the same level of care and expertise.

One partner for every surface in your home. That's not just a service — that's a promise.
